Waffles

Description

Waffles are delicious breakfast or dessert treats loved by many. They are made from a batter consisting of flour, eggs, milk, and butter, which is cooked in a waffle iron until golden and crispy on the outside. The result is a delightful combination of fluffy texture on the inside and a slightly crispy exterior. Waffles can be enjoyed plain, but they are often topped with a variety of tasty additions such as syrup, powdered sugar, fresh fruits, whipped cream, chocolate sauce, or even savory options like fried chicken or bacon. Whether enjoyed as a traditional breakfast or a delightful indulgence, yummy waffles are a beloved treat that satisfies both the taste buds and the soul.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3⁄4 cups milk
  • 1⁄2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon salt
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Nonstick cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions.
  2. Whisk eggs in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Add flour, milk, and vegetable oil and mix to combine. Whisk in sugar, then mix in baking powder, salt, and vanilla just until smooth, being careful not to overmix.
  3. Spray the preheated waffle iron with nonstick spray. Pour batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook until golden brown and the iron stops steaming, 3 to 5 minutes.
